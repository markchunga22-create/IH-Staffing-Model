Staffing & Burnout Risk Model

Burnout risk is modelled as a weighted composite of six operational variables: patient volume (30%), sick call rate (20%), seasonal index (20%), shift length (20%), consecutive shift days (15%), and unit acuity (25%). Weights are calibrated against CIHI 2024 staffing benchmarks and cross-referenced with the Maslach Burnout Inventory's known risk factor structure. FTE requirements are derived from nurse-to-patient ratios adjusted for acuity and sick call buffer.

Estimates only, not actual Interior Health operational data. Costs use BC Ministry of Health 2024/25 benchmarks.
