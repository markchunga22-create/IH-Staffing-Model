Staffing & Burnout Risk Model

Burnout is scored using six operational inputs. Volume and sick call rate carry the most weight because they directly determine the ratio of workload to capacity. Shift structure and acuity adjust the score based on how work is distributed and how cognitively demanding it is. The six weights sum to 100% and are cross-referenced against CIHI 2024 benchmarks and the Maslach Burnout Inventory.
